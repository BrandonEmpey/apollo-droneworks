SAFETY GUIDELINES:
- Maintain visual line of sight at all times
- Avoid flying in restricted airspace
- Do not fly in adverse weather conditions
- Keep minimum 50ft distance from people and property
- Check battery levels before each flight
- Calibrate compass before flying in new locations
